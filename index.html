The Program

The hackNY Fellows program pairs the best technical minds with the greatest New York startups.

The summer Fellows program pairs exceptional quantitative and computational students with the best startups in NYC. Students enjoy free housing together and a pedagogical lecture series to introduce them to the ins and outs of joining and founding a startup.
